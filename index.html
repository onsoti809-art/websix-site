FAQ

Questions, answered.

How long does a website take?
Most Starter and Professional sites launch in about 2–4 weeks once we have your content. E-commerce and custom builds take longer — we'll give you a clear timeline in your quote.
Do I own my website and domain?
Yes — always. We set up your domain and hosting in your own accounts and hand over everything, including the code. Your website is your asset, not ours.
I don't have a website yet. Can you help?
Absolutely — that's our specialty. We'll handle everything from domain and design to launch, and explain each step in plain language.
Will my site work well on phones?
Every site we build is mobile-first, fast and accessible, so it looks and works great on phones, tablets and desktops.
Will it help me show up on Google?
We build in SEO fundamentals — clean structure, fast load times, proper tags — and set up analytics so you can track results. Ongoing SEO is available as an add-on for more competitive markets.
How does payment work?
After your free audit we send a fixed, written quote. Typically a deposit starts the project and the balance is due before launch. No hidden fees.
What happens after launch?
You can take a care plan (from $100/mo) for updates, backups, security and support — or take full ownership with training and documentation. It's your call.
